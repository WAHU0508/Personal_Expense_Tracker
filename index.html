FAQs

Q:What is Bucks Buddy?

A:Bucks Buddy is a simple expense tracking app that helps you manage your expenses and stay on top of your finances.

A:Bucks Buddy uses a simple and intuitive interface to allow you to log your expenses and categorize them for easier reference. The app then provides a detailed report and visualization to help you understand your spending habits
